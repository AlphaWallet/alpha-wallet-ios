How does selling work?

Create a listing on our marketplace by setting a price in ETH (Ethereum) and quantity. Once your listing has been sold, you will receive the ETH in your wallet.

What is Ethereum?

ETH is short for Ethereum. Ethereum is a crypto currency (similar to BitCoin) running on blockchain technology.
